# nada — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 9, 2026

nada is a dopamine-placebo wellness app. The short version: **we collect nothing.**

## Data collection

nada does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no
accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking SDKs, and no third-party
data processors.

## What stays on your device

Everything the app shows you — money "saved," streaks, intercept history, and time
reclaimed — is computed locally and stored only on your device (in the app's local
storage). It never leaves your phone. Deleting the app deletes all of it.

## Network use

The app loads product and feed imagery from a public image CDN (images.unsplash.com)
over HTTPS. These requests carry no personal information or identifiers beyond what any
standard image download includes (your IP address, handled per the CDN's own policy).
No purchases occur in nada — checkout is intentionally fake; no payment information is
ever requested, entered, or processed.

## Children

nada does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it does
not collect data from anyone.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if optional sync is added someday), the new
policy will be posted here before the change ships.

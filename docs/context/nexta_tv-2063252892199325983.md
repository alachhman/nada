## Concept summary

A "dopamine website" / habit-simulator: it recreates the *ritual and anticipation* of an impulsive habit — online food ordering, shopping, a smoke break — and delivers the dopamine hit of the lead-up, while deliberately removing the payoff (no order placed, no money spent, no cigarette smoked). The product is essentially a **harm-free placebo for compulsive behaviors.**

Core mechanics observed in the SK trend:
- **Fake food delivery:** browse menus, read reviews, fill a cart, "track a courier" — but you can't check out.
- **Virtual smoke break:** anonymous chat rooms where strangers socialize, recreating the social-break feeling without nicotine.
- **Goal:** familiar dopamine hit without spending money, smoking, or giving in to impulse.

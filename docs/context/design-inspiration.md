## Synthesized design language for nada

A **warm, comfy, premium-but-friendly** marketplace with **Amazon-density content** rendered in a **clean modern store** skin and **award-calibre motion**.

- **Palette:** cream/off-white base (`#F7F4EE`-ish), espresso ink for type, soft pastel accents (peach, sage, butter, lilac); elegant near-black surfaces reserved for photo/hero and the intercept "magic moment."
- **Type:** big, bold, rounded headlines; generous whitespace; quiet secondary text.
- **Shape & depth:** large corner radii, soft layered shadows, **tactile product imagery** that feels liftable.
- **Motion (Reanimated/Moti + haptics):** spring physics, count-ups, scan/glow reveals, sticker/badge pops, sheet transitions. Motion is a feature, not decoration.
- **Surfaces:** rounded pill CTAs, soft cards, smooth bottom sheets, segmented progress.
- **Navigation:** native iOS **bottom tab bar**.
- **The signature moment:** the checkout **intercept** becomes the app's "Apple-Design-Award" beat — processing theater → spring + confetti/glow reveal of "You saved $X," with haptics — the emotional payoff the whole product is built around.

## How this changes nada

The store becomes the home (Amazon-dense, beautiful). The old "dopamine dashboard" (savings, streak, recent saves) moves to a secondary **You/Saved** tab. The intercept mechanic is unchanged in logic but elevated into a delightful animated moment.
